Platform Features

Built for enterprise-grade
fund administration

Every feature has been designed to address real operational challenges faced by pension fund administrators.

🔐
Role-Based Access Control
6 distinct roles — Admin, FundAdmin, Member, Employer, InvestmentOfficer, Compliance — each with carefully scoped API and UI access. Every endpoint is JWT-protected.
📊
Real-Time Ledger
Every transaction — contributions, interest, claims, annuity disbursements — is instantly recorded in the member ledger with full audit trail. Members see their own view, admins see all.
⚡
Claim Workflow Engine
Structured 4-step claim lifecycle: Submitted → UnderReview → Approved → Disbursed. Supports 7 claim types including Retirement, Housing, and Disability.
💰
Annuity & Pension Payouts
Create LifeAnnuity, JointAnnuity, TemporaryAnnuity, and GuaranteedAnnuity plans. Process monthly disbursements with TDS calculation and complete payment history.
📈
Investment Portfolio Tracking
Track fund allocation across 5 asset classes: Government Securities, Corporate Bonds, Equity, Fixed Deposit, and Money Market. Visual pie chart allocation view.
📑
Statutory Compliance Reports
Generate statutory returns by period, track contribution defaults, and browse the full audit trail with entity-type and date range filters for regulatory submissions.

Workflow

From enrolment to pension
in 6 steps

The complete PF lifecycle, automated and auditable at every stage.

1
Create Scheme
Admin configures EPF, Gratuity or Superannuation scheme with contribution rates and interest.
2
Enrol Members
Employers enrol employees. Fund accounts are auto-created and linked to schemes.
3
Process Contributions
Employers submit monthly remittances. FundAdmin reconciles and posts to ledger.
4
Credit Interest
Annual interest is calculated and credited to every member's fund account.
5
Process Claims
Members submit claims. FundAdmin reviews, approves and disburses with tax calculation.
6
Pay Pension
Retirees receive monthly annuity payments with full TDS deduction and history tracking.

  • Create schemes, employers, members
  • Process and reconcile remittances
  • Manage all claims and annuity plans
  • View audit trails and reports

  • Reconcile contribution remittances
  • Review, approve, disburse claims
  • Credit annual interest to accounts
  • Create and manage annuity plans
  • View compliance reports

Member

  • View personal fund balance
  • Track contribution history
